Cheese Burger

Juicy beef patty topped with cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, and our special sauce served in a toasted bun. Perfect for a quick lunch or a tasty dinner.

Serves: 2
Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 beef patties
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 burger buns, toasted
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the beef patties with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill patties on medium heat for 4-5 mins per side.
  3. Place cheddar slices on patties while hot to melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble burger: bun, lettuce, patty, tomato, sauce, top bun.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
