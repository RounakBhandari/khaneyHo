Comfort Foods with a Healthy Twist

Love burgers, fries, and pizza? Learn how to make guilt-free, nutritious versions of your favorite comfort foods without compromising on taste.

Baked Sweet Potato Fries

Instead of deep-fried potatoes, slice sweet potatoes, toss with olive oil, paprika, and bake until crispy. Full of vitamins and fiber, but still crispy and delicious!

Cauliflower Pizza Crust

Swap regular dough for a cauliflower-based crust. Bake until golden and top with tomato sauce, veggies, and light cheese for a nutrient-packed pizza night.

Grilled Veggie Burgers

Replace beef patties with black bean or lentil patties. Grill them lightly, top with avocado, lettuce, and whole grain buns for a hearty but healthy burger.

Zucchini Noodles (Zoodles)

Replace traditional pasta with spiralized zucchini. Toss with your favorite sauces and toppings. Lower carbs, more fiber, and just as satisfying!

Baked Mac & Cheese with Hidden Veggies

Boost your macaroni with pureed cauliflower or butternut squash in the cheese sauce. Creamy, comforting, and sneaky veggies included!
