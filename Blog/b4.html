5 Spices That Transform Any Dish

Sometimes, a pinch of spice can take a recipe from bland to brilliant. Discover the secret of cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, paprika, and chili flakes and learn how to use them in everyday cooking.

Cumin

Earthy and warm, cumin adds depth to curries, stews, and roasted vegetables. Toast the seeds lightly to release its aroma before adding to dishes.

Turmeric

Bright yellow and slightly bitter, turmeric enhances rice dishes, soups, and smoothies. Combine with a pinch of black pepper to maximize health benefits.

Cinnamon

Sweet and warm, cinnamon works in both desserts and savory dishes. Sprinkle on oatmeal, roast vegetables, or mix into stews for subtle flavor enhancement.

Paprika

Sweet, smoked, or hot, paprika adds vibrant color and a mild peppery taste to meats, soups, and roasted vegetables. Adjust type based on desired heat and flavor.

Chili Flakes

Add a kick of heat to any dish with chili flakes. Perfect for pizzas, pasta, soups, and stir-fries. Sprinkle at the end of cooking to retain spiciness.
