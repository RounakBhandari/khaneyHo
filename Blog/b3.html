Budget-Friendly Meals for Busy Weeknights

Quick and affordable meals that save time and keep everyone satisfied. Learn how to plan, prep, and cook tasty weeknight dinners on a budget.

One-Pot Veggie Pasta

Cook pasta with seasonal vegetables, garlic, and canned tomatoes in one pot. Top with cheese for a quick and filling meal.

Chicken and Rice Skillet

Sauté onions, garlic, diced chicken, rice, and broth. Simmer until cooked, season with herbs or paprika.

Lentil and Veggie Stir-Fry

Cook lentils, stir-fry onions, bell peppers, and carrots. Mix lentils with soy sauce and spices for a protein-packed meal.

Sheet Pan Fajitas

Slice chicken and bell peppers, toss with oil and fajita seasoning. Roast for 20-25 minutes. Serve with tortillas and salsa.

Baked Potato Bar

Bake potatoes and top with beans, cheese, sautéed veggies, and a dollop of yogurt for a versatile, low-cost dinner.

Egg Fried Rice

Use leftover rice, sauté onions and garlic, scramble eggs, then mix rice and frozen veggies. Ready in 15 minutes!

Turkey or Veggie Chili

Cook ground turkey or skip for vegetarian, add beans, tomatoes, chili powder, and simmer. Serve with rice or bread.

Pasta Salad with Tuna

Mix cooked pasta with canned tuna, chopped veggies, olive oil, and lemon. Serve cold or at room temperature.
